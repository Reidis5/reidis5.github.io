Music Producer/Engineer

My SoundCloud

One of the things that I am most passionate about in life is music. I start producing music a little under a year ago, and I have had so much fun learning new tricks from fellow producers as well as experimenting with new sounds on my own. I make a variety of beats including pop, alternative rock, and hip hop, and I will occasionally do edits of songs I like!

Collaborating with other artist is definitely my favorite part of what I do. It's so much fun learning how another persons style might fit with your own and how the two of you can merge those styles to create something new altogether.

In addition to making music, I have directed a few music videos for other artists, and it is always a blast watching a song be brought to life by the artist who wrote it. There is an inspiring level of energy present when a passionate artist is able to show the world how they feel about their own art.
